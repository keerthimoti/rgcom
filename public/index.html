About Us

RG Auto Components Private Limited, headquartered in Chennai, INDIA, was founded in the year of 2010 as a manufacturing unit. Today it has expanded its boundaries to sourcing and supply chain, engineering a wide range of fasteners items for the Automotive and Industrial segments world-wide. RG is powered by top breed people having extensive knowledge on Heavy Automobiles Manufacturing and schooled from India’s top 50 Engineering colleges.RG is not built with brick, mortar and machines, rather it is built with highly talented and intrinsically motivated associates bringing with them an enormous amount of experience raring and sharing their expertise to empower the company to achieve greater heights. RG’s foundation is laid strong on its Core principles of “Customer satisfaction with Quality, Cost and JIT delivery”.

RG is an ISO 9001-2008 Certified company, certified by TUV SUD.

Our Mission

RG as a company understands and values every customer’s timely needs and expectations. Putting together its widespread functional and focused technical expertise towards delivering results on time, with high quality and great precision is the prime and the foremost mission of the organization. This mission of RG is achieved by putting to practice some of the best practices like JUST IN TIME, Reduced Time to Market approaches into its Supply Chain work process.
